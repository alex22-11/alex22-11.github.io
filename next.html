Alex's guide to shrimp keeping

So what's next?

There isn't actually much left to do. At this point you just have to do regular maintenance, being:

  • Regular water changes (20% once a week)
  • Removal of algae from plants
  • Scrubbing algae from glass
  • Vacuuming gravel
  • Checking water parameters

From here on, you just have to regularly check your water parameters (pH, kH, gH, ammonia, nitrite and nitrates) to know when your tank will be ready for shrimps. By the time you see ammonia levels dropping, you should be able to start putting in some snails to do the cleaning for you.
