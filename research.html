What to look for

Most elements like decoration is completely optional, but there are a few essentials listed below.

Before decorating the aquarium to your taste, you obviously need to get some supplies. Here is a list of essential supplies so you know what you will need to buy online or in your local fish store

Essentials

  • An aquarium (The larger the better. Keep in mind that you'll have to spend more on substrate, filtration and plants)
  • Substrate
  • Filtration
  • Plants
  • Lights
  • Heater
  • Fertilizers
  • Water testing kits
    • Ammonia
    • Nitrites
    • Nitrates
    • Iron
    • Phosphate
    • gH and kH (only if you want to keep caridina shrimp)
  • Algae eating snails
  • Shrimp food to start the nitrogen cycle
